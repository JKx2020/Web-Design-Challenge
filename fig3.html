STEP 3: Your personalized map!

After we auto-generate hundreds of possible locations for a vacation we then narrow down the list based on your weather preferences. We ask for your desired temperature, cloudiness, windiness, and humidity ranges and start narrowing down the list of cities that fit your ideal vacation weather. In the example here, we narrowed down our locations by:
  • A max temperature lower than 90 degrees but higher than 70
  • Wind speed less than 10 mph
  • Zero cloudiness
This map is a heat map based on temperature in each location. The more red the heat-map, the hotter it is.
