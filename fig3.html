STEP 3: Your personalized map!

After we auto-generate hundreds of possible locations for a vacation we then narrow down the list based on your weather preferences. We ask for your desired temperature, cloudiness, windiness, and humidity ranges and narrow down the list of cities to fit your ideal vacation weather. In the example here we narrowed down our locations by:

  1. A max temperature lower than 90 degrees but higher than 70
  2. Wind speed less than 10 mph
  3. Zero cloudiness
This map shows the current temperature in the citities that fit the above criteria. The more red the heat-map, the hotter it is!
